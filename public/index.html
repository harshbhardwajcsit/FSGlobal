Our Story

the three musketeers all from engineering background came up with this idea of promoting a healthy lifestyle. We realized that the first and foremost thing that would help one keep healthy is food. They realized some modifications needs to be done in our daily food intake to help limit the calorie intake to ones body. This could be done with by preparing healthy plus tasty meals. But again in this busy working life of Pune, it could be a little difficult for everyone to manage their time and come eat the appropriate meals. So we decided to deliver food meal boxes at everyone’s doorstep to avoid inconvenience. In this way, a journey began of coming up with a company which would deliver food at your doorsteps. Soon the team of three starting with our founder and CEO Ms.Prachi Jha and co-founders COO Mr.Suraj Dinda and CFO Mr.Gaurav Bagwe established FOODSWAP and started their venture of delivering healthy meal boxes. On their journey, they were accompanied by their dietitian and expert chefs. So come along and join us on our journey of creating a healthy lifestyle without compromising on taste or habits.
